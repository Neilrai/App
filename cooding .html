Meet Our Founder

Coding Academy was founded by Mr. Avanish Garkoti, a visionary entrepreneur with over 10 years of experience in the field of coding. Mr. Garkoti’s vision is to empower the next generation with coding skills and eliminate unemployment by equipping them with the tools needed to thrive in the tech industry.At Coding Academy, Mr. Garkoti is dedicated to providing the highest quality coding education and training to individuals of all ages and backgrounds. He believes that coding is an essential skill that everyone should possess, regardless of their career aspirations.Under Mr. Garkoti’s leadership, Coding Academy has grown into a leading institution in the field of coding education. His unwavering commitment to his vision has earned him the respect and admiration of his peers and the wider community.

Mr. Garkoti’s passion for coding education and his dedication to empowering the next generation of coders has become the driving force behind Coding Academy’s success. His leadership and guidance continue to inspire the team at Coding Academy to push the boundaries and develop innovative solutions to the challenges facing the tech industry.Overall, Mr. Garkoti’s entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to his vision of a world where no one is left behind has positioned Coding Academy as a leader in the field of coding education.
